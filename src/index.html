Welcome to ID 290 Interactive Design II

The primary scope of the Interactive Design II course is to facilitate an intermediate understanding of design principles, creativity and innovation
as translated through various web-programming languages.

Meet the Instructor: Paul Edoho-Eket

Originating from a fine arts educational background, my experience comes with 8 years of graphic industry related work sub-contracting for several prominent design firms and Universities. I have been a graphic and/or web design educator for over 5 years and continue to do that currently. I enjoy web application development, interactive design, motion graphics and visual effects, graphic design, as well as fine and studio art.I find the creation process as a whole highly rewarding, and look forward to assisting others delineate their creative agendas.

Objective

In this class we will learn to create interactive design with tools such as HTML5, CSS3, jQuery AJAX and JavaScript.
